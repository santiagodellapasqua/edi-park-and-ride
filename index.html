Park and ride at Edinburgh Airport is a parking service where you drive to an off-airport car park (0.5-2 miles from the terminal), park your car, and catch a complimentary shuttle bus to the airport. The process is:

  1. Drive to the park and ride car park
  2. Park in any available space (you keep your keys)
  3. Catch the free shuttle bus to Edinburgh Airport terminal (2-15 minute journey)
  4. On return, catch the bus back to your car
